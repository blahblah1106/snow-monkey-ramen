THE STORY OF OUR RAMEN

Who are we?

Snow Monkey Ramen is a unique ramen shop that was found by two snow sport advocates who crossed paths in the beautiful mountain slopes of Hakuba, Japan. Their shared passion for skiing and appreciation for the authentic flavors of Japanese cuisine have inspired them to create a unique dining experience. At Snow Monkey Ramen we blend the cooking techniques of French and Japanese to bring you a new unique taste of our ramen.
